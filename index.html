The Bouquet of Wonderville

A whimsical children's book about unicorns, rainbows, sunshine, candy, and the magic of being different

About the Book

Deep in the magical world of Wonderville, a little unicorn named Luna Sparklehorn dreams of creating the happiest bouquet ever imagined. With the help of her colorful friends—Ziggy the zebra, Mimi the flamingo, Gerald the giraffe, and many more—the bouquet blossoms with joy, sunshine, gummy bears, and rainbows.

"The Bouquet of Wonderville" is a delightful, 20-page children's picture book that celebrates diversity, friendship, and the beauty of sharing joy with others. With its whimsical characters and hidden life lesson, it's a gentle, heartwarming tale perfect for young dreamers.
